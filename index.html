01. About

Who I Am

I'm Harrison Wekesa Wanjala — an ICT lecturer, Android developer, and content creator based in Kitale, Kenya.

I teach ICT at Kitale National Polytechnic, where I develop curriculum, run labs, and build software tools that serve the institution's 12,000+ students directly.

On the development side, I specialize in native Android apps using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, and full-stack web systems using the LAMP stack. I don't build tutorial clones — every project I ship solves a real operational problem.

Outside of code and class, I run a tuk-tuk business, explore small-scale farming, and produce faceless video content on TikTok and YouTube around tech education and storytelling.
